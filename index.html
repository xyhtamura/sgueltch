soft errors, virtual ooze — rotware for organic, symbiotic glitch

a word mash of squelch and glitch, this is an aesthetic philosophy and software suite. This proposes a philosophical shift in digital aesthetics, moving away from traditional glitch art's rigid, pixelated, and mechanical disruptions toward organic, fluid, and biologically-inspired distortions. It values asymmetry, unpredictability, and embodiment over algorithmic repetition, taking the mechanics and aesthetics of traditional glitch and moving it from rigidity to organicity.

Pixel Lesions

Infect an image with organic growths; lesions spread and sort local pixels into living distortions.

SiltCRT

A soft granular signal emulator: Voronoi phosphors, oozy turbulence, chromatic bleed, bloom & umbra.

Gurgulator

A granular resynthesizer specializing in gurgling noises, with erratic pitch bends, time warps, burping filters, and gooey convolution reverb.
